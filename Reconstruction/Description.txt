
Posterior_samples.csv:
======================================================================

Column 1: Year

Columns 2..101: 100 posterior draws of the global annual average temperature series.



Posterior_summaries.csv:
======================================================================

Column 1: Year

Column 2: Posterior mean for that year

Column 3: Posterior SD for that year

Column 4: Posterior 0.025 quantile for that year

Column 5: Posterior 0.975 quantile for that year
